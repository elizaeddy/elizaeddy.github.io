Spun wool weaves through fabric, beginning to connect history and culture with texture and meaning.

 

Telling stories of old for the modern world. To reconnect and for those to take further. Undone. Not perfect. Almost, perhaps. Basic in lines and forms. True in how the symbols read. Perhaps this is perfect. Perhaps not, but a start. This is what I strive to create with my handwork — a start, or a direction back to the beginning.  Inspired by the places I live — the mountains of Montana and the Atlantic Ocean.

 

Hand stitched pillows with spun wool on linen in a burlap weave. Down insert.

Hand stitched wall pieces on linen framed in vintage finds or hand built wooden frames.
